POMELO COLLINS

Alcoholic Cocktail glass

Juice your pomelo with any means possible and strain the juice Mix all the ingredients in a shaker with ice Shake and strain in a glass tumbler over fresh ice

Ingredients
  • 50 ml gin
  • 80 ml pomelo juice
  • 20 ml lime juice
  • 40 ml honey syrup *
